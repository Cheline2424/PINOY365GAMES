Responsible Gaming

Commitment to Our Players

At PINOY365, we believe that gaming should always be a fun and entertaining activity. We are committed to promoting a responsible gaming environment for all our members in the Philippines. Gambling should never be seen as a way to make money or pay off debts. We encourage all users to play within their means and maintain control over their gaming habits.

Naniniwala kami sa PINOY365 na ang gaming ay dapat manatiling masaya at nakakaaliw. Kami ay nakatuon sa pagtataguyod ng responsible gaming para sa lahat.

How to Stay in Control

To ensure your gaming remains enjoyable, please follow these guidelines:

  • Set a Deposit Limit: Never deposit more than you are prepared to lose.
  • Don't Chase Losses: If you lose money, do not try to win it back by betting more.
  • Keep Track of Time: Monitor how much time you spend on gaming apps.
  • Balance Your Life: Ensure gaming doesn't interfere with your work or family responsibilities.

Recognizing the Signs

If you find yourself answering "Yes" to these questions, you may need to reconsider your gaming habits:

  • Do you skip work or school to play?
  • Do you borrow money to gamble?
  • Do you feel depressed after losing?
  • Is gaming your only hobby?

Where to Seek Help

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling addiction, please reach out to professional support organizations in the Philippines such as PAGCOR's responsible gaming programs or local counseling services.

Remember: It is okay to ask for help.
