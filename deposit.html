Tutorial Guide

Follow this 6-step visual guide to fund your PINOY365 account. We offer the fastest processing for GCash, Maya, and USDT to ensure you never miss a winning moment.

1

Login to Your Member Account

The first step is to access your dashboard. Enter your correct username and password on our secured login page. This ensures all your subsequent transactions are linked to your personal wallet.

Description: Gambar ini menunjukkan tampilan utama portal login PINOY365. Pastikan URL Anda valid sebelum memasukkan data pribadi untuk keamanan akun yang maksimal.

2

Locate the Deposit Icon

Once logged in, look for the 'Deposit' or 'Wallet' button. On mobile devices, this is typically found at the bottom navigation bar or at the top right header for instant access.

Description: Tampilan ini memberikan gambaran jelas posisi tombol deposit di dalam dashboard member. Navigasi kami dirancang agar pemain baru dapat menemukan menu cash-in dalam hitungan detik.

3

Choose Payment Category

Our system automatically categorizes payment methods into E-Wallets/Local Banks and Crypto. Choose the one that best suits your needs for speed and convenience.

Description: Gambar katalog metode pembayaran ini menunjukkan pilihan luas yang tersedia. Pemain dapat beralih antara metode tradisional Filipina atau metode crypto global sesuai preferensi.

4

Option A: GCash, Maya, or Local Bank

For the majority of PH players, this is the best choice. Simply enter your desired amount (Min ₱100), select your provider, and follow the on-screen prompts to complete the transfer via your e-wallet app.

Description: Bagian ini merinci formulir pengisian saldo melalui GCash dan Maya. Masukkan nominal yang diinginkan dan sistem akan memberikan nomor tujuan transfer secara otomatis yang harus Anda salin.

5

Option B: USDT Crypto (TRC20 Network)

Ideal for high-volume players. Select USDT, and the system will generate a unique TRC20 wallet address. Send the exact amount from your Binance or Trust Wallet for a borderless, secure transaction.

Description: Tutorial visual untuk deposit crypto menggunakan Tether (USDT). QR Code dan alamat wallet yang muncul sangat unik dan terenkripsi untuk menjamin dana Anda masuk tepat sasaran ke akun PINOY365.

6

Check History & Confirm Success

After completing the transfer, go to your transaction history. Once the status changes to 'Success', your credits are ready to use. If any delays occur, screenshot this page for support.

Description: Contoh tampilan riwayat transaksi sukses. Pantau setiap dana yang masuk secara transparan melalui menu history ini untuk memastikan saldo akun Anda telah bertambah sesuai transfer.

Deposit FAQ & Tips

How fast is the processing?

Normally, GCash and Maya are instant. USDT may take 1-3 minutes depending on blockchain confirmations.

What is the minimum amount?

For most local methods, the minimum starts at ₱100. For USDT, please follow the minimum value shown on the payment page.
